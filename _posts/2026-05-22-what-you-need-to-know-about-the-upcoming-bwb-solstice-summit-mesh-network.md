We are setting up an **experimental mesh network** at the upcoming **BWB Solstice Summit**.

This network test is part of our ongoing work to support community-led, off-grid communications and to learn what works in real event conditions.

## Event Snapshot

* **Dates:** June 18-21, 2026
* **Main event window:** Thursday, June 18, 2026 at 12:00pm to Sunday, June 21, 2026 at 12:00pm PDT
* **Location:** Fly Ranch (Hualapai Flat, Fly Ranch County Rd 34), Gerlach, Nevada
* **Theme:** Living Ritual

## Why This Matters

Fly Ranch is an off-grid site with limited solar power and no cell reception. That makes this summit a perfect environment for balancing radical self-reliance with communal effort by building a mesh network for decentralized communication.

Our goal is to test setup patterns, participant onboarding, and practical use cases for short-message coordination in a remote environment.

## What To Expect From The Mesh Experiment

* Coverage and performance will vary based on terrain, placement, power, and participant devices.
* This is an experiment, so we expect to learn and iterate in real time.
* We will share practical guidance for participants who want to join the network test.

## Summit Notes

The BWB Solstice Summit at Fly Ranch is a four-day campout focused on stewardship, collaboration, and participant-led programming.

Participants are asked to practice radical self-reliance and Leave No Trace, and to coordinate logistics through the summit registration process after ticket purchase.
